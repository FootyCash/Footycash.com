About Footy Cash

Fantasy Sports is a multi-billion dollar industry that gained popularity in the United States, but is quickly taking Europe and Australia by storm. As the industry grows, so does the demand for legitimate payment options. Footy Cash will serve as a safer and faster alternative. Gambling online usually requires entering personal info and credit card numbers. Footy Cash will make it easier to deposit on fantasy sports websites, saving you time and money in the process.

Fantasy Sports

Footy Cash is going to target new fantasy sports websites in Europe and Australia. Having additional payment options is a good selling point for customer, and it also saves the company on processing fees that come along with credit cards or Paypal.

Lower Fees

The coin was created so that people can safely gamble on sports online. In the United States, fantasy sports websites have become extremely popular, with Draft Kings and Fan Duel worth with 1 billion dollar valuations. Footy Cash is going to be a payment option on popular sports websites, allowing customers to gamble with lower fees and faster transactions.

Active Development

Gambling websites like Fan Duel and Draft Kings are popping up in Australia and Europe where Footy is huge. Footy Cash is going to be ahead of the curve, developing the market from the very beginning! We are actively working on creating an app, getting added to new exchanges, and working with up and coming fantasy sports websites in Europe and Australia.
